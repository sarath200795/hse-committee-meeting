## Full narration (plain text)

Welcome to HSE Committee Meeting. Run safety committee meetings — minutes, attendees, CAPA actions and a statutory compliance calendar.

In this short tutorial, we'll walk through how to use the app.

First, register your organization. The first person to sign up becomes the administrator.

Enter your details and a password, then create the organization.

Meetings dashboard. Meeting summary tiles, stats and analytics by type and status.

Meetings list. Browse and search all logged meetings; open one for full minutes and actions.

Facility sites. Meetings are scoped to sites for statutory compliance tracking.

Team & approvals. Approve sign-up requests and manage member roles.

HSE Committee — consultation, on the record.. Start by registering your organization.

That's a quick tour of HSE Committee Meeting. Thanks for watching — get started by registering your organization.
